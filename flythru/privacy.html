Cookies

We use "cookies" on certain pages of our websites.

"Cookies" are small files placed on your hard drive that can help us analyze the flow of our web page, provide customized services, deliver content and advertisements, measure the effects of offers, and enhance trust and security.

Some important information you should know about "cookies" are:

  • We offer some real features that are only available through the use of cookies.
  • Cookies help us identify the user while maintaining access.
  • Most cookies are "session cookies", meaning that they are automatically removed from the hard proximity at the end of the cycle.
  • You are free to remove our cookies if your browser permits, although this may interfere with some of our websites and services.
  • You may encounter third-party cookies on other pages and websites that we do not control (for example: if you browse to another user's website, they may place cookies on your hard drive).
